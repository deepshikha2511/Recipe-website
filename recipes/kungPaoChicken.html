Kung Pao Chicken

Ramen Dish

A stir-fried dish with chicken, peanuts, vegetables, and chili peppers in a savory and slightly spicy sauce.

Ingredients for Kung Pao Chicken:

For the marinade:

  • 500g boneless, skinless chicken breasts or thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

For the sauce:

  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch

Other ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup unsalted peanuts
  • 3-4 dried red chili peppers (adjust to your preferred spice level)
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 cup diced onions
  • 3-4 green onions, chopped (white and green parts separated)

Steps:

  1. Marinate the chicken
  2. Prepare the sauce
  3. Heat the oil
  4. Stir-fry the chicken
  5. Toast the peanuts and chili peppers
  6. Add garlic, ginger, and vegetables
  7. Combine everything
  8. Garnish and serve
